# Superpotential corrections from Euclidean D3-branes on abelian C^3 orbifolds

## Background
- In the work of /references/D-instanton-induced-superpotential, the authors show how to compute overall normalization of ED3 corrections to superpotential of CY3 orientifolds compactifications
- The result is written in terms of Annulus (boundary on D-instanton-D-instanton and D-instanton-spacefilling tadpole-compensating brane) and Mobius strip (boundary on D-instanton) partition functions 
- We wish to perform the CFT exercise of computing these partition functions for the case of C^3/Gamma orbifolds and orientifolds thereof
- To start with, we wish to focus on Gamma abelian, with the main example being C^3/Z3, where the Z3 acts via (x,y,z) ~ e^{2 pi i/3}(x,y,z).
- This orbifold has a crepant resolution into O(-3) over CP^2 (denote fiber as p)
- There are two orientifolds we can perform for this example (given an involution)
    * [x,y,z,p] -> [x,y,z,-p], fixed point is p = 0, which is the exceptional divisor (the CP^2). This gives O7 wrapping the exceptional divisor.
    * [x,y,z,p] -> [-x,y,z,p], fixed point is x = 0 or y=z=p=0 (remember, we are in CP^2 now), this gives either O7 wrapping O(-3) over CP1 i.e. a noncompact O7 or a spacefilling O3
